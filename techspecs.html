  LED

Loochi led is nothing like the leds you have seen before.
We have chosen LedEngin's LuxiGen technology which provides an amazing amount of light in all sorts of colors!
For the more technically enclined, Loochi is based on a 10W RGB leds. which provide approximately as much power as a 40W bulb.
Those leds are rated for over 100,000 hours lifetime which is over 11 years!

  OpenSource hardware

Loochi hardware and software is completely open-source, including the most original part: the high-power current-switching led drivers.
We are very happy to be part of a broad movement that brings open source to the world of hardware and can not wait to see what new objects will be built on top of our designs!
